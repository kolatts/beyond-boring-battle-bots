# App Icon — Brilliant's Head

**For:** The square app icon / favicon for the game.

**Aspect ratio / size:** 1:1 square, generate at 1024x1024; must stay readable when scaled down to 32px (keep the head large, centered, minimal detail at the edges).

## Prompt

```
hand-inked retro-futurist mascot robot art, thick cream sticker-style outlines, subtle paper grain and ink texture, warm palette — ink #161412, cream #f5efe0, dim paper #d8d2c2, orange #e87722, bright orange #ff9a3c, beige #b9b1a0, danger red #c0392b — CRT-screen faces with glowing features, soft neutral gray studio background unless the prompt says otherwise, no photorealism, no text/lettering unless the prompt explicitly asks for it.

square app icon: ONLY the head of BRILLIANT, a cheerful hero robot, perfectly centered and filling most of the frame, straight-on front view, rounded cream-white head casing with a thick ink outline, big rectangular dark CRT-screen face, two glowing bright-orange four-pointed starburst eyes, huge open friendly grin glowing orange, single orange bobble antenna poking up from the top edge, tiny orange side ear-caps, simplified bold shapes with minimal interior detail, flat warm cream background disc behind the head, generous even margins on all four sides, no body, no hands, no props, iconic, high contrast, instantly readable at 32 pixels
```

## Variations

- Dark-mode alternate: near-black ink background disc, head rim-lit in bright orange.
- Winking variant: one starburst eye swapped for a happy crescent, for notification badges.
- Flat two-color cut: pure ink and orange only, no cream, for monochrome / favicon-16 contexts.
