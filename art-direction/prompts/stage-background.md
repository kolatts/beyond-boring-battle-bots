# Stage Background — Purgatorial Open-Plan Office

**For:** The wide fighting-stage backdrop behind the two fighters during quiz rounds.

**Aspect ratio / size:** 21:9 ultrawide preferred (e.g. 2520x1080); 16:9 at 1920x1080 acceptable. Keep the center-bottom third empty of props.

## Prompt

```
hand-inked retro-futurist mascot robot art, thick cream sticker-style outlines, subtle paper grain and ink texture, warm palette — ink #161412, cream #f5efe0, dim paper #d8d2c2, orange #e87722, bright orange #ff9a3c, beige #b9b1a0, danger red #c0392b — CRT-screen faces with glowing features, soft neutral gray studio background unless the prompt says otherwise, no photorealism, no text/lettering unless the prompt explicitly asks for it.

background scene, no characters: an ultrawide fighting-game stage backdrop of a purgatorial open-plan office at dusk, endless rows of gray-beige cubicle partitions receding into dim haze on both sides with one-point perspective, cold fluorescent light strips running along a low dark ceiling, a few strips flickering, tangled ethernet cables and sagging motivational-poster frames left blank, a lonely water cooler, dead potted plant, wheeled office chairs abandoned mid-roll, drifting loose paper on the carpet tiles, on the far left one tall window glowing with warm orange sunset light cutting a single hopeful beam across the floor, the wide center foreground floor completely empty and level — clean open carpet stage space reserved for two fighters, subtle halftone shading in the shadows, painted-backdrop depth like a 90s arcade fighter stage, deadpan corporate purgatory with one warm ember of hope
```

## Variations

- Boss-round variant: dusk turns to night, fluorescents go red-tinged, the far conference room glows ominously at center-back.
- Victory variant: blinds open, warm orange light flooding every aisle, papers settling like confetti.
- Add tiny background spectators: two or three faceless gray office bots peeking over cubicle walls.
