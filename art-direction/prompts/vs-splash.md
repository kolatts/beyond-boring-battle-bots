# VS Splash — Brilliant vs Boring

**For:** The pre-fight versus screen shown before each round begins.

**Aspect ratio / size:** 16:9 landscape, generate at 1920x1080 or larger (keep the exact center clear for a "VS" emblem overlay added in the app).

## Prompt

```
hand-inked retro-futurist mascot robot art, thick cream sticker-style outlines, subtle paper grain and ink texture, warm palette — ink #161412, cream #f5efe0, dim paper #d8d2c2, orange #e87722, bright orange #ff9a3c, beige #b9b1a0, danger red #c0392b — CRT-screen faces with glowing features, soft neutral gray studio background unless the prompt says otherwise, no photorealism, no text/lettering unless the prompt explicitly asks for it.

classic fighting-game versus-screen split composition, a jagged hand-inked lightning crack tearing vertically down the middle of the frame dividing two halves, LEFT HALF on a warm cream-and-orange burst background: BRILLIANT, a cheerful hero robot, rounded cream-white shell with orange trims, rectangular CRT-screen head with glowing orange starburst eyes and huge confident grin, orange bobble antenna, orange play-button triangle chest sigil, leaning into the frame in 3/4 view with a raised chunky fist, sparks flying; RIGHT HALF on a cold desaturated gray-beige background: BORING, a corporate villain robot, desaturated gray-beige shell, heavier blockier frame, boxy CRT-screen head with half-lidded deadpan glowing eyes and flat line mouth, dull red bobble antenna, laminated ID badge, arms crossed, staring back utterly unimpressed; both busts large and face-to-face across the crack, halftone speed lines radiating from the center, small open diamond of empty space at the exact center of the crack reserved for a VS emblem, high-energy arcade drama meeting immovable corporate apathy
```

## Variations

- Include a chunky beveled "VS" monogram in the center diamond (orange glow, ink outline) instead of leaving it empty.
- Boring's half rendered slightly grainier and dimmer, like a screen losing signal.
- Mirror-friendly alternate: both bots in strict profile silhouette for a more graphic, poster-like read.
