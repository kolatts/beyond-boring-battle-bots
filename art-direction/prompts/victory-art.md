# Victory Art — Boring Defeated

**For:** Full-screen celebration art shown when the player wins the game.

**Aspect ratio / size:** 16:9 landscape, generate at 1920x1080 or larger.

## Prompt

```
hand-inked retro-futurist mascot robot art, thick cream sticker-style outlines, subtle paper grain and ink texture, warm palette — ink #161412, cream #f5efe0, dim paper #d8d2c2, orange #e87722, bright orange #ff9a3c, beige #b9b1a0, danger red #c0392b — CRT-screen faces with glowing features, soft neutral gray studio background unless the prompt says otherwise, no photorealism, no text/lettering unless the prompt explicitly asks for it.

triumphant knockout scene: in the lower right, BORING, a corporate villain robot with desaturated gray-beige shell, heavy blocky frame, boxy CRT-screen head and dull red bobble antenna, short-circuited and collapsed face-up into a toppling mountain of TPS reports, manila folders, binder clips and triplicate forms, limbs splayed, little hand-drawn smoke curls and fizzling spark ticks rising from his joints, his red antenna bobble cracked and dangling, his dark screen face showing only a tiny glowing orange error glyph, laminated ID badge sliding off the paper pile; in the upper left, BRILLIANT, a cheerful hero robot with rounded cream-white shell, orange trims, rectangular CRT-screen head with glowing starburst eyes and an enormous open grin, orange bobble antenna, orange play-button triangle chest sigil, frozen mid-jump in a joyful victory leap, one chunky fist punched to the sky, legs tucked, radiating hand-drawn orange starbursts and confetti-like paper scraps, halftone sunburst behind the hero, dynamic diagonal composition from defeated pile up to leaping winner, upbeat arcade win-screen jubilation
```

## Variations

- Boring giving a weak thumbs-up from inside the paper avalanche — beaten but a good sport.
- Confetti made entirely of shredded meeting agendas and approval forms.
- Close crop on Brilliant's leap for a portrait-orientation share image, paper mountain reduced to the bottom edge.
