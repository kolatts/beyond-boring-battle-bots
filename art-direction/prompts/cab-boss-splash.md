# Boss Splash — The Change Advisory Board

**For:** Full-screen splash art introducing the final boss round.

**Aspect ratio / size:** 16:9 landscape, generate at 1920x1080 or larger.

## Prompt

```
hand-inked retro-futurist mascot robot art, thick cream sticker-style outlines, subtle paper grain and ink texture, warm palette — ink #161412, cream #f5efe0, dim paper #d8d2c2, orange #e87722, bright orange #ff9a3c, beige #b9b1a0, danger red #c0392b — CRT-screen faces with glowing features, soft neutral gray studio background unless the prompt says otherwise, no photorealism, no text/lettering unless the prompt explicitly asks for it.

dark dramatic boss-introduction splash: BORING, a corporate villain robot with desaturated gray-beige shell, heavy blocky frame, boxy CRT-screen head with half-lidded deadpan glowing eyes and flat line mouth, dull red bobble antenna, laminated ID badge — now transformed into THE CHANGE ADVISORY BOARD, enlarged to looming boss scale, wearing a danger-red ceremonial sash across the chest, enthroned in a towering high-backed executive chair behind a long curved conference-table dais, one massive fist raising a wooden gavel high overhead about to slam a verdict, flanked left and right by rows of faceless featureless dark robot silhouettes seated in judgment, their screens blank voids, cold fluorescent light strips glowing overhead in a low dark ink ceiling, red rim lighting on the boss shell, papers and sticky notes drifting like ash, low worm's-eye camera angle looking up at the dais, deadpan corporate horror, oppressive committee-meeting menace, final-boss gravitas
```

## Variations

- Gavel mid-slam with an orange shockwave ring and scattering "denied" paperwork.
- Tighter portrait crop: just Boring's sashed bust and gavel, silhouettes reduced to dark shapes at the frame edges.
- A single tiny warm-orange glow (Brilliant, off-frame) reflected in the dark glass of Boring's screen face.
